## Usage

1. Open the app in development mode (`npm run dev`)
2. Click the purple Dev Menu button in the bottom-right corner
3. Expand the menu
4. Click on any of the Quick Login buttons to instantly log in as that role

Note: The Dev Menu only appears on localhost/127.0.0.1 in development mode.
